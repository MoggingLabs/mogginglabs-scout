# Privacy and Compliance

Scout is designed around source tracking, suppression controls, export/delete workflows, and approval-gated outreach.

## Required controls

- Store source metadata for lead data.
- Do not send outreach without approval.
- Maintain suppression lists.
- Include unsubscribe/compliance controls before email sending is enabled.
- Keep audit logs for high-risk actions.
- Support export/delete workflows.

## CAN-SPAM note

Commercial email in the United States has compliance requirements, including accurate sender information, non-deceptive subject lines, a valid physical postal address, opt-out mechanisms, and honoring opt-outs.
