# Waterfall Plan

MoggingLabs Scout follows a waterfall process.

The app becomes fully usable only at final acceptance. Intermediate phases are construction milestones, not partial product releases.

## Phase gates

1. Requirements and product scope
2. Architecture and provider decisions
3. UX/UI system design
4. Database, RLS, API, audit, and test plan
5. GitHub project setup
6. Implementation foundation
7. Lead data core
8. CRM workflow
9. Map intelligence
10. AI analysis
11. Outreach approval and reply tracking
12. Admin, compliance, billing-ready controls
13. Release candidate and final acceptance

## Explicitly excluded

Route planning, route optimization, route export, and physical visit workflows are out of scope.
